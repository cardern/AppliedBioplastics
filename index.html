The Global Plastic Problem

It can take 1000 years for plastic to degrade. This creates several environmental issues, some of which include soil infertility, water system blockages, fetal birth defects, non-potable water, and threats to both land and aquatic life.

There is an unsustainable amount of single-use plastic production with no viable plan to dispose of it. While we still need to find a way to deal with the existing amount of plastic in the ecosystem, our solution will allow consumers to dispose of their biodegradable bags without concern.

Frequently asked questions

What products do you make?

Our flagship product offering will be the BioBag. This is a fully biodegradable replacement for traditional plastic bags with 1.5 times the load bearing capacity.

How can I purchase your products?

We are currently gearing up our manufacturing process for the BioBag and expect to be entering the market within 6-9 months. If you are interested in a wholesale purchase, please contact us.
